World cup journey

**English:** Lionel Messi’s World Cup journey is one of the most inspiring in football. He made his debut in **2006**, showing early promise as a young talent. In **2010**, he played a major creative role but didn’t score. The **2014 World Cup** became a turning point—Messi carried Argentina to the final, won the Golden Ball, but narrowly lost to Germany. In **2018**, Argentina struggled and exited in the Round of 16. Finally, in **2022**, Messi completed his greatest triumph. He delivered heroic performances, scored crucial goals, and led Argentina to win the **FIFA World Cup**, securing his place as one of the greatest players in history.
